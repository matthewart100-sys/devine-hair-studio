Meet Jennifer

A master colorist with the eye of an artist and the patience of a specialist.

Jennifer Devine is an award-winning master colorist and stylist based in Denver, Colorado. A 1999 Pivot Point/Aveda graduate, she specializes in custom color, color corrections, natural blondes, dimensional blonding, luxury color services, extensions, cuts, keratin treatments, and smoothing treatments.

Her work is personal by design. No color mixture is the same; she tailors formulas, products, timing, and service plans to each client's unique hair chemistry, history, and goals.
