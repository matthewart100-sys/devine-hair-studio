Behind the chair

She treats every client's hair with respect, care, and love.

Jennifer attended Pivot Point and Aveda, graduating in 1999. Her specialties include color corrections, extensions, highlights, balayage, custom color, cuts and trims, keratin treatments, smoothing treatments, natural blondes, and luxury blonding.

No color mixture is the same. Jenn individually tailors products, formulas, timing, and service plans to meet each client's unique hair chemistry needs.
